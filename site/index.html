02 / ASSURANCE KERNEL

Hard rules stay hard.
Even in Light mode.

Review cost may change. Source identity, verified license facts, media rights, privacy, accountable authorship, key facts, provenance, attribution, and publication-clearance gates do not.

  1. 01

    Preflight before public use

    A deterministic scan captures the authoritative rights page, asserts known-source facts, records separate AI-use notices without rewriting the license, and issues a hash-bound receipt. Private authoring stays open; publication receives verified facts and explicit warnings.

  2. 02

    Evidence, not a remembered label

    Raw snapshots, hashes, canonical URLs, retrieval metadata, exact source revisions, and separate media-rights receipts bind each decision to inspectable evidence.

  3. 03

    Accountable public authorship

    Public output names a final responsible non-AI person or institution, explicitly confirmed by the user. Placeholder identities and tools cannot masquerade as a rights holder.

  4. 04

    Focused critique in every mode

    Light mode uses a separate same-model pass for identity, licenses, attribution, media rights, and scientific key facts. Full mode uses cross-model review. Both records are hash-bound and become stale after edits.

  5. 05

    Fail-closed release

    Packing rebuilds carriers against an identified human visual review and refuses unresolved rights, provenance, attribution, source-comparison, accessibility-floor, contract, or coherence failures.

HONEST BOUNDARY

Coursewerk finds the question.
It does not invent the answer.

A publisher’s AI-use notice is not automatically a copyright-license term. The pipeline preserves it with legal effect undetermined. Private work remains open; public preparation verifies the source facts and gives the instructor the warning and final decision.

05 / CLAIMS WITH EDGES

Automation is useful.
Overclaiming is not.

AUTO

Coursewerk can verify

  • Package layout and required files
  • Rights evidence and declared obligations
  • Hashes, provenance, attribution, and links
  • Syntax and defined accessibility proxies
  • Structural alignment and revision freshness
HUMAN

Educators still decide

  • Factual and calculation correctness
  • Pedagogical fit for actual learners
  • Legal interpretation and permissions
  • Alt-text quality and assistive-tech behavior
  • Final browser, print, and projector aesthetics
