02 / ASSURANCE KERNEL

Hard rules stay hard.
Even in Light mode.

Review cost may change. The source identity, process restrictions, license, privacy, authorship, provenance, attribution, and publication-clearance gates do not.

  1. 01

    Preflight before ingestion

    A deterministic scan captures the authoritative rights page and detects generative-AI or automated-processing notices. An unresolved condition stops the workflow.

  2. 02

    Evidence, not a remembered label

    Raw snapshots, hashes, canonical URLs, retrieval metadata, extracted text, and known-source assertions bind each decision to inspectable evidence.

  3. 03

    Accountable public authorship

    Public output names a responsible non-AI author or institution. Tools may be disclosed; they cannot masquerade as a rights holder.

  4. 04

    Fail-closed release

    Packing refuses unresolved rights, provenance, attribution, source-comparison, accessibility-floor, contract, or coherence failures.

HONEST BOUNDARY

Coursewerk finds the question.
It does not invent the answer.

A publisher’s process-specific notice is not Coursewerk’s policy. The pipeline records and surfaces it before the AI workflow proceeds, then requires permission or an accountable qualified decision.

05 / CLAIMS WITH EDGES

Automation is useful.
Overclaiming is not.

AUTO

Coursewerk can verify

  • Package layout and required files
  • Rights evidence and declared obligations
  • Hashes, provenance, attribution, and links
  • Syntax and defined accessibility proxies
  • Structural alignment and revision freshness
HUMAN

Educators still decide

  • Factual and calculation correctness
  • Pedagogical fit for actual learners
  • Legal interpretation and permissions
  • Alt-text quality and assistive-tech behavior
  • Final browser, print, and projector aesthetics
